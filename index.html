Introduction

Hello there! My name is Kyle Shi, and I'm excited to welcome you to my website. Here, you'll get to know me a little better and explore some of my interests. I was raised in New York City's Lower East Side neighborhood, and I'm an Asian American with roots in both China and Taiwan. I'm currently a senior at High School for Dual Language and Asian Studies, and I'm passionate about exploring new ideas and concepts. I am participating in 2023 All Star Code Summer Intensive a program to help teach coding languages like HTML, Javascript, and CSS and internship skills to young men of color. Thanks for stopping by, and I look forward to connecting with you further!

My skills

  • - Proficient in Python
  • - Beginner-level skills in CSS, JavaScript, and HTML
  • - Strong time management and organizational skills
  • - Creative and innovative thinker
  • - Skilled at problem-solving and adapting to new challenges
  • - Customer Service Skills

My passion

My passions range from the athletic field to the digital world of technology. Playing American football has been a joy of mine since childhood. The teamwork, strategic thinking, and discipline required to bring fulfillment that extends beyond the game. As a fan of the New York Giants, I appreciate and idolize how Saquon Barkley exemplifies hard work, grit, and perseverance on and off the field. In my free time, I also enjoy video gaming and computer programming. First-person shooter games test my video game skills while coding challenges my problem-solving abilities through constantly learning new languages and concepts. The intellectual stimulation and accomplishment of overcoming complex challenges feed my curiosity and drive me forward. Overall, my interests stem from a desire for both physical and mental stimulation. Whether on the field, in front of a screen, or with lines of code, I find purpose in activities that push me to grow, earn and work through obstacles with resilience and good spirit.

My goals

I am a very ambitious person who loves to see the world change for the better. My primary focus in society is to aid in eradicating world hunger. I demonstrate this dedication by volunteering at food pantries, ensuring that people within the NYC communities get the necessities needed to live day by day. My ultimate goal is to utilize my software development skills to find a way to contribute to the issue of world hunger, whether it be in a small or significant way.
